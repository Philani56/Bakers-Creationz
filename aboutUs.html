About Us

About Us

At Bakers Creationz, we are passionate about baking the finest, freshest, and most delicious products. Our journey began with a simple idea – to bring joy and quality to our customers through handcrafted bakery goods made with the utmost care and the finest ingredients. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for every customer. Our mission is to delight our customers with high-quality bakery products and outstanding service. We strive to innovate and exceed expectations, making every visit to Bakers Creationz a memorable one. From our oven to your door, we create delicious memories with everything from celebration cakes to Snowballs. Pure delight in every bite! Founded by Njabulo Sithole, Bakers Creationz started as a home-based venture in Inanda, Durban, driven by a passion for baking and a keen sense of market opportunity. What began with baking for family and friends quickly expanded into a thriving business. Njabulo's major breakthrough came in 2012, leading to a move to a dedicated facility to accommodate growing demand. Today, we proudly supply leading South African supermarkets, including Pick n Pay Express, BP, Engen garages, and select Spar Supermarkets in KwaZulu Natal. With our recent national contract with Pick n Pay, we are set to extend our reach across the country. Our commitment extends beyond just baking. We continuously strive to improve our processes and innovate our offerings to ensure that every product meets the highest standards of quality. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a treat, Bakers Creationz is here to make every moment sweeter. At Bakers Creationz, the home of good baking, we are dedicated to crafting exceptional products that bring joy and satisfaction. Our dedication ensures that each creation is a testament to quality and craftsmanship, delivering pure delight in every bite.
